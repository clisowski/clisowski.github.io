Clayton Lisowski grew up a video game enthusiast. Some of his earliest memories include playing Pokemon on his Nintendo DS. The photo found on this page is Clayton's brand new HP Omen gaming desktop. He used to be a proud Xbox gamer, but with the convincing of his older brother, he decided to upgrade and get a new PC with specs that allow for him to play his favorite games with friends.
